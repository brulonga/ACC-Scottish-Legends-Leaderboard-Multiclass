* Net Pos Gained: The total number of positions a driver has gained (+) or lost (-) compared to their theoretical Pace Position across the season.

* To be classified, drivers must complete at least 70% of the leader's laps. Drivers who do not meet this are excluded from these standings.

* Nürburgring 24h race is excluded from 'Avg Pace Gap' due to its unusually long lap time.

* Incidents: Laps that fall outside 107% of the session's overall best lap.
